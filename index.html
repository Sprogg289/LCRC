Welcome, Staff Member.

This portal serves as the primary resource for LCRC staff. Our community thrives on professionalism, respect, and high-quality roleplay.

Community Highlights & Values

Community First

LCRC is built on the passion of our players. As staff, our goal is to facilitate fun while maintaining order and safety for everyone.

Staff Integrity

We expect every staff member to act with honesty. Admitting mistakes and learning from them is how we grow as a team.

RP Excellence

Promoting immersive storylines and realistic interactions. Staff should guide players toward better roleplay habits.

Rules & Fairness

The rules apply to everyone equally. No favoritism, no exceptions. We maintain a level playing field for all users.

Active Support

Being a staff member means being helpful. Answer questions, guide new players, and be a positive force in the server.

Mutual Respect

Treat every player with the respect you expect in return. De-escalation is our most powerful moderation tool.
